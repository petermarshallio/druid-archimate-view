Enterprise Architects ()
An EA is concerned with making sure that the company’s entire toolset remains a benefit to the whole company – that the cost and the value delivered are well-balanced, and that risks are minimised. They encourage participation in IT decisions to maximise utility across the business and is interoperable with other ecosystem components, avoiding costly technical diversification and proliferation. They want to make sure tools are compliant and support business continuity and warranty requirements. They are concerned with protecting the company's IP and its data, ensuring data can be added with confidence, has integrity and is adequately protected while stored, is accessible to those that need it, and can be effectively disposed of.
